The cloud revolution signifies a major shift in how businesses approach IT infrastructure, moving away from traditional on-premises solutions to flexible, scalable, and cost-effective cloud services. This transition is not just a technological evolution but a business revolution, enabling new ways of thinking and new business models. Cloud computing offers classic benefits such as cost reduction, quick implementation, usage-based payment, increased flexibility and scalability, and security according to standards and norms, enhancing competitiveness through modern IT solutions. It also allows for pilot projects in larger IT solutions to gather experience and benefits various departments and areas within a company.
