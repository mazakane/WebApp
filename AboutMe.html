About Me
Hello! I'm Markus, the creator of this blog.

I'm passionate about the cloud, and I love sharing my thoughts and experiences with you.

On this blog, you'll find articles on various cloud topics, as well as helpful tips and tricks. If you'd like to learn more about me or have any questions, feel free to reach out. I look forward to hearing from you!
Enjoy the moment before it becomes a memory.

This is a motto that holds true now more than ever, as it's essential to seize the moment, to savor it, to truly "live" it. So why not do so in the cloud as well? A contradiction? I don't think so.

With my years of experience in IT and the underlying (infrastructure) components, I've come to the conclusion that it's not a contradiction at all. Whether in industry, healthcare, or even in our private, deeply personal lives, the cloud is an ideology that can no longer be dismissed.

And yes, a thoughtful, perhaps even ethically-based approach to the cloud is necessary—now more than ever. That's precisely why, in my opinion, refusing to engage with it is the wrong approach. By doing so, you miss the chance to understand these systems and even more so, the opportunity to use them, instead of being used by them.

Be that as it may, with my experience as a Cloud Architect, Certified Trainer, and Solution Specialist, I've had numerous encounters with the cloud, and I believe it's high time we place our bets on a horse that's been in the stable for a long time, just waiting to finally demonstrate its capabilities and potential.

This blog will be a part of that journey.

I look forward to enjoying the moment.
